BLACKPINK’s Jennie Trends Worldwide Following
Appearance At Chanel Fashion Show

Posted on May 22, 2019 at 12:00 PM

On October 1, BLACKPINK’s Jennie attended the Chanel show at Paris Fashion Week, and sat in the front row
next to Cardi B and Anna Wintour! Dressed in blue wide-legged pants, a blue cropped top with a large bow at
the front and a short lavender cardigan, Jennie had all eyes on her with her signature cute and classy vibes.
She waved to fans and media with her adorable gummy smile, and made even grown women around her caress her
like a baby with her lovable charms!

At the conclusion of the fashion show, Jennie posted to her Instagram, showing how much fun she had before,
during and after the event!Last year, CHANEL picked Jennie as their brand ambassador and model for its cosmetics
line Chanel Beauty. Appropriately nicknamed “Human CHANEL”, Jennie truly carried the brand’s name with grace and
striking visuals that show how right they are for each other!
