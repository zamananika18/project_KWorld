SuperM Makes Highly-Anticipated Debut With “Jopping” MV

Posted on September 8, 2019 at 12:00 PM

SuperM has made their debut!
As a new group from SM Entertainment, SuperM brings together SHINee’s Taemin, EXO’s Baekhyun and Kai,
NCT 127’s Taeyong and Mark, and WayV’s Lucas and Ten into one group. “Jopping” is an electro-pop track
with a grandiose and energetic sound, and has the message, “Let’s burn it up on the stage to make it
our own and enjoy it.” The song will put all of the members’ talents on full display. SuperM’s first
mini album will be released on various Korean music streaming sites at 6 p.m. KST, and they will
perform together for the first time at Capitol Records in Hollywood on October 5 local time.
