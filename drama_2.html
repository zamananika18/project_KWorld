Lee Sung Kyung In Talks For Leading
Role In “Romantic Doctor Kim” Season 2

Posted on May 22, 2019 at 12:00 PM

On September 9, a source from Lee Sung Kyung’s agency YG Entertainment stated to MyDaily, “Lee Sung Kyung
has received an offer for ‘Romantic Doctor Kim’ season two and is positively looking over it.” A source
from SBS also relayed the same statement. Actress Lee Sung Kyung will reportedly be leading the second
season of SBS’s “Romantic Doctor Kim”! Reports emerged on September 9 that Lee Sung Kyung will most likely
be working with Han Suk Kyu and Ahn Hyo Seop. Han Suk Kyu is in talks to make a return after the first
season while Ahn Hyo Seop was reported to be in talks to join the drama back in June. Lee Sung Kyung is
set to take on the role of Cha Eun Jae, who is a second year fellow in the cardiosurgery department.
From a young age, she was often called a genius and was on an elite path with praise and expectations
from those around her. Although everything seemed easy and clear in her textbooks, she freezes once she
steps into the operating room but she undergoes changes under the watchful eye of her mentor played by
Han Suk Kyu. “Romantic Doctor Kim” was a bit hit when it aired from 2016 to 2017, telling the real
stories of medical professionals and connecting with viewers. If she agrees to play the role, this
will be Lee Sung Kyung’s first medical drama in three years since she appeared in the 2016 drama
“Doctors.” The second season of “Romantic Doctor Kim” is set to premiere in December.
