TXT Introduces Comeback Concept For “The Dream Chapter: Magic” With Psychedelic Trailer

Posted on October 3, 2019 at 12:00 PM

TXT shared a concept trailer for their return with “The Dream Chapter: MAGIC”! After a trippy and impressive
dance performance by the TXT members, Huening Kai approaches the camera and asks, “Shall we run away?”
Pre-orders for “The Dream Chapter: MAGIC” begin on October 2. This will be TXT’s first comeback since
they made their debut in March with “The Dream Chapter: STAR“!
