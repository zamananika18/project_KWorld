MAMAMOO’s Wheein Takes Over Major
Realtime Charts With “Good Bye”

Posted on May 22, 2019 at 12:00 PM

MAMAMOO’s Wheein is topping charts with her new track!
On September 4 at 6 p.m. KST, Wheein released her solo song “Good bye” produced by Jung Key off her album “soar.”
As of September 5 at 1:30 a.m. KST, “Good bye” has scored the No. 1 spot on the realtime charts of major Korean
music sites Melon, Genie, Bugs, Naver, and Soribada, as well as No. 2 on Mnet.
Congratulations to Wheein!
