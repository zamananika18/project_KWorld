AB6IX Is Gearing Up For October Comeback

Posted on May 22, 2019 at 12:00 PM

AB6IX is getting ready for their first comeback! On September 4, it was announced that AB6IX is
gearing up in preparation for a new album with the goal of releasing it in early October. The
five-member Brand New Music rookie group made their debut in May with “Breathe.” They recently
wrapped up their first Asia fan meeting tour “1ST ABNEW,” with stops in six cities including
Seoul, Tokyo, Singapore, Taipei, Hong Kong, and Bangkok.
